Send a Path Payment

Sends a payment to another account using a different source currency than the currency the destination receives. If there's an offer on the network for the source currency / destination currency, the payment can be sent, otherwise it needs to go through paths of more than 1 offer. This is why you need to specify a send max, the amount of source currency to send may fluctuate based on what offer is selected.
